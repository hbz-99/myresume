My FAQ

Tell me more about yourself

I'm a very simple person to be honest. If i want something, I'll do what is needed to get it. I also play sports on the side with Basketball being a favourite. It helps me focus when things get tough.  

I love to draw as well but never took it to the professional level as my colorblindness has hindered me from taking any design courses. It never got me down though. 

The important thing about me is that im very wiling to learn to be the best. Critism is much looked down upon by people, but i believe that we need to accept it to be a better version of ourselves.

Do you have a set of rules to stand by?

Be Straight.
I dont like to beat around the bush, usually I'll get straight to the point and would really appreciate the honesty of people around me as well.

There's always 2 sides to a coin.
In my years in the service industry, I learned the hard way that to every story there's always more than 1 side. It helped me greatly in solving problems and now I try to view things from another's point. It helps me see a clearer picture of any situation.

Treat everyone with respect.
This is a simple act which I think that, people, in all honesty, forget. If you want to be treated with respect, it is only natural to give respect as well. 




Why did you choose to leave the Hospitality Industry?

While I could see myself progressing further in the industry, I quickly realised that it wasn't what I wanted for myself.  After serving National Service, I found it even less rewarding to be in the industry. Here, I decided to pursue what i originally wanted before, Marketing. 

I simply couldn't stand giving good service standards to people who don't deserve it. I took a step to leave the Hospitality scene by taking up a course in Digital Marketing. 

What kind of person do you hope to be?

In the future, I hope to take the skills and start my own business. I want to be a person that people pook for to improve their presence online. I want to be that one guy who can inspire others to go and chase their dreams.  

Do you have a portfolio to show your skills in Photoshop or Illustrator?

Im fairly new to the programs. Everything that I've done so far has been self taught. As my creations increase, ill post it up on the site. The logo you see is one particular example. 

But why Digital Marketing?

NS changed my views. Simple as that. 

When i was serving with the Police Force, we all had secondary duties assigned to our team. My team in particular was in charge of the station's social media account. From there I picked up Photoshop as i was assigned with the creation of the media. 

I quickly grew fond of it and decided to further myself and picked up Illustrator as well. This was the beginning of a discovery of my new self.
